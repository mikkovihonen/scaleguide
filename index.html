Scaleguide

IntroductionGuide

Scaleguide is a site for beginners in music theory. On this site you'll find basic infomation about different musical scales and chord progressions presented visually.

Later on this might expand to musical notation, but right now the focus of this site is just to provide the different scales and degrees.

It is also possible to print the different scales for offline use. Please use Google Chrome if you wish to print the guides. Also make sure the "Print background graphics" setting is checked under "more settings" in the Google Chrome print dialog.
